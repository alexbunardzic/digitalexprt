Episode 8 With Alex Weidauer

My friend Alex Weidauer is the founder of Berlin-based startup Rasa. His company makes open source tools that enable businesses to automate conversations with their customers. When Alex and I met last summer we spent some time discussing the current situation and also imagining what will the near future bring. We met again today to discuss all the salient points that conversation automation encompasses.


Recorded on January 24, 2018.
