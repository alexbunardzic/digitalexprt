Miguel Fernandez, Claimsbot (Part 1)

Miguel Fernandez is the product developer at Claimsbot. The chatbot Miguel is developing specializes in processing insurance claims. If you take the time to listen to what Miguel has to say about this chat service, you will realize that the proverbial rubber hits the road at the point where customers, who are already fully engaged, get to enjoy the added value of getting things done by interacting with Claimsbot.


This is part one of the multi-part series where Miguel and myself delve deeper into the discovery of what is possible to accomplish using what Miguel aptly named 'disposable apps'. Please stay tuned for the subsequent episodes where we reveal some of the amazing features of the Claimsbot. Recorded on January 4, 2018.
